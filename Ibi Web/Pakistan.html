MORs as a sign of Prestige

The native blue (Indian) peafowl has always been a symbol of beauty, luck and holiness in Pakistani society. There are traditional dances which depict the dance of peacock. MORs are found in the south eastern desert of Pakistan as well as in the Cholistan desert of Punjab.MORs are also kept as pets and are thought to be sign of pride for the more affluent socio-economic classes of Pakistan.

The MOR Utility Market

MOR beauty is widely celebrated in Pakistan and there is a booming industry of MOR products online, on store shelves, as well as showcased by street vendors. MOR inspired wedding and part themes are consistently in-style as the MOR beauty is a source of inspiration for many.

MOR-inspired truckart

MOR appreciation is symbolic of all art forms in Pakistan, but displays a particular zeal in the traditional 'truck art' niche. MORs are painted on countless pick-up trucks and industrial delivery vehicles accross the nation and continue to inspire unique art pieces and home decor items like china work and other display items.
